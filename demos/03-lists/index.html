How To Make Honey Muffins
  1. Mix flour, suger, and baking powder in a small bowl
  2. Mix eggs, Honey, oat milk, and vanilla in a separate large bowl
  3. Add dry mixture to the liquid one in to batches, mixing thoroughly
  4. Distribute mixture into greased muffin tins
  5. Bake for 16 minutes, or until lightly browned
  6. Enjoy!
